Addition is the most basic form of math (besides counting). This tutorial will teach you the basics of Standard Algorithim.

Standard Algorithim is when you put one number on top of another one:
15+4= can be re-written as
15
+ 4

Where you put the answer is under the line.

The first thing you do is add the ones place (5 and 4) which is 9. Then, (if you need to carry the one add 1 to the tens place) add the tens place (1 and 0), which is 1. The result is 19!
